Mobile Check Deposits. Banks nowadays often offer a mobile check deposit feature that lets customers deposit a check through the bank's app using a mobile device with a camera. ﻿ The feature makes it easy to get money into your account without going to the bank. The Amounts Are Not Unlimited In order to limit a bank's liability if someone makes a mobile deposit in one bank and physically deposits or cashes the check in another bank, there are maximum mobile deposit amounts. The limits are generally from USD $1,000 to $2,000 per day and from $3,000 to $5,000 per month. See mobile payment service, online banking and virtual bank.
